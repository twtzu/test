Bali

A captivating Indonesian island paradise, is a symphony of natural beauty and vibrant culture that beckons travelers worldwide. With its lush emerald rice terraces, pristine beaches kissed by azure waters, and mystical temples that seem to touch the sky, Bali is a haven for those seeking enchantment.

Funfacts You Need To Know About Bali

5,780 km2 administrative area and includes the island of Bali, Nusa Penida, Nusa Lembongan, and Nusa Ceningan.

4,317,404 locals at the 2020 census with about 86.91% of Bali's population adheres to Balinese Hinduism.

#1 ranking in the Travelers' Choice Awards in 2019 by TripAdvisor, #2 ranking in 2023.

More than 400.000 international tourist coming monthly in 2023.*
